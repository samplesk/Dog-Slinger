About Us

Hi! See this guy? ——-> (cartoon - bitmoji style Josh picture imagining different dogs) Josh is his name. He’s a local fella, who spent his entire life dreaming of having a hot dog joint,or a hot dog stand, or a hot dog cart, or a hot dog truck. Imagine Bubba from the Forrest Gump movie, only Josh imagines all kinds of hot dogs instead of shrimp dishes: chili dogs, cheese dogs, Chicago Dogs, New York Dogs, Seattle Dogs, Hawaiian Dogs, and on and on. I think you get the picture. This dude loves hot dogs. So one day this hot dog dreaming prince met the partner of his dreams, Cori, pssst that’s me ———-> (cartoon - bitmoji style Cori picture). Cori grew-up in Chicago and always dreamed of having a specialty-food business, too. Together these two foodies, cooked and created some pretty unique hot dogs. It was red hot magic! (Cartoon - Bitmoji us cooking like mad scientist in a kitchen making magic) So when this amazing opportunity came along to open their own hot dog joint, they jumped at the chance. (Cartoon - Bitmoji us jumping Hurray! here) So here they are living the dream, creating delicious franks. The DogSlinger menu includes all the the standards that you’re already familiar with, specialty gourmet-inspired dogs for the more adventurous, and pub-inspired sides…think of an order of loaded tots that are big enough to share. Currently DogSlinger does not offer eat-in service. However, we have to-go service to take home or eat in our side-yard, or if you’re not comfortable coming inside we offer pick-up car-hop service. For a fee, we deliver to local downtown Village of Stevensville businesses with our golf cart. (Cartoon - Bitmoji us in front of the DogSlinger)
